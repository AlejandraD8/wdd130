Colombia

Colombia is my country. I was born in Boyacá, one of the most beautiful departments in my country, I have lived here all my life and I like it, it is a quiet place, its climate is temperate and it is not expensive. I am proud to say that I am Colombian because I love my country, it is very beautiful and has many good places to visit, wonderful and friendly people, a great variety of flora and fauna, and delicious food. So I would like people to know what is really good about Colombia and not be guided by stereotypes.
